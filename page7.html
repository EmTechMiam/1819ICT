MS Powerpoint Basic Tutorial

Look for the MS Powerpoint in the taskbar or Search on the Start Programs

MS POWERPOINT Interface

Inserting a new slide

Go to the Home Tab and click on New Slide.

Choose the Layout required, the layouts are pre-set but can be customised as needed.


Changing Fonts, Size, Format

Go to the Home Tab and Look for the symbols similar on the right.

Highlight the objects or word before selecting any of the commands.

Inserting Transitions and Animations

1. Click the Transitions Tab and click the slide then choose the desired transition.

2. Click the Animations Tab and highlight the words or objects you want to be animated then select animations

.

Inserting a Diagram

1. Choose INSERT >> Smart Art

2. Choose DIAGRAM TYPE >> OK

3. Click on the objects within the diagram to edit them.
